Terrazu Café, like our namesake, the Tarrazu region of Costa Rica, world famous for the exquisitely balanced coffee grown there, balances our gourmet coffee shop with our café serving creative and healthy sandwiches and salads to an ever increasing health conscience market. Terrazu Café prepares a full range of the finest gourmet coffees, teas, world famous Davidovitch bagels, pastries and breads as well as exquisite desserts, all prepared by local roasters and bakeries. Our menu of sandwiches and salads, all made fresh to order, are uniquely created using only the finest products, and procured from local sources whenever possible, with a strong emphasis on fresh and healthy.
